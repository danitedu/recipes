3 eggs
1 cup of milk
1 cup of sugar
2 cups of flour
1 tbsp vanilla extract
100 grams butter
150 grams chocolate chips

Mix sugar and butter till creamy. 
Add eggs and incorporate them. Vanilla extract. 
Add milk and flour taking turns.
Last add the chocolate chips.
Pour the mix in a cooking tin.
Cook at 180 degrees for 30 minutes.
